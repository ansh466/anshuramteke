Privacy Policy

Last updated: July 16, 2026

Arrow Rush ("we", "our", or "the App") respects your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains what information the App may collect, how it is used, and your choices.

1. Information We Collect

The App may collect limited technical information such as device type, OS version, crash logs, and advertising identifiers through third-party ad SDKs (for example, Google AdMob) to show ads and improve stability.

We do not require you to create an account, and we do not intentionally collect personal identity information such as name, email, or phone number.

2. Game Progress

Local progress — including unlocked levels, scores, settings, and rating preference — is stored on your device using local storage. This data stays on your device unless you clear app data or uninstall the App.

3. Advertising

The App uses Google AdMob (or similar partners) to display ads, including:

  • App-open ads (first launch)
  • Interstitial ads
  • Rewarded ads

Ad partners may use device identifiers to serve relevant ads. You can reset or limit ad personalization in your device settings.

Google’s privacy policy: https://policies.google.com/privacy

4. Analytics & Crash Reporting

We may use anonymous analytics or crash-reporting tools to understand performance and fix bugs.

5. Children's Privacy

The App is a general-audience casual game. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children.

6. Data Sharing

We do not sell your personal information. Advertising and platform SDKs may process data under their own privacy policies (for example, Google).

7. Your Choices

  • Mute music and sound effects in Settings
  • Reset progress by clearing app data
  • Manage ad personalization from your device advertising settings

8. Changes

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Continued use of the App after an update means you accept the revised policy.

9. Contact

For privacy questions about Arrow Rush (com.puzzle.arrowrush), contact the developer via the Google Play Store listing support email.
